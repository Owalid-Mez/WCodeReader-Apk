# WCodeReader v1.0 / v1.1

**WCodeReader** is a fast and reliable Android app to **scan barcodes and QR codes**, manage your products, and export your product list to CSV.  
It provides a simple, professional interface with **Material Design components** and enhanced usability.

With **v1.1 Advanced Mode**, you can now manage detailed product info, including prices, manufacture & expiry dates, and receive alerts for products nearing expiration. You can also use it as a complete **inventory tool** on your Android device — no separate barcode scanner needed.

---

## Features

### v1.0 (Initial Release)
- Scan **barcodes and QR codes** using your device camera.
- **Create or upload** a product database (CSV/JSON).
- Automatically **load product names** from your database.
- **Add products manually** if not found in the database.
- **Delete scanned items** individually with the **Material delete button**.
- Color-coded list:
  - **Green**: exists in database  
  - **Red**: not in database  
  - **Blue**: manually added
- **Export scanned products** to CSV.
- Toolbar includes a **Close button**.
- Works **offline** with no internet required.
- Built with **CameraX**, **ML Kit**, and **Material Design**.

### v1.1 (Advanced Mode)
- Track **Buying & Selling prices** for each product.
- Record **Manufacture & Expiry dates** with alerts for expiring items.
- Load CSV/JSON databases with **advanced fields**.
- Toggle between **Normal Mode** (basic) and **Advanced Mode** (detailed).
- **Update your database** directly from the app and export changes.
- Enhanced CSV export including all advanced fields.

---

## Usage

1. Open the app on your Android device.
2. *(Optional)* Upload your product database (CSV/JSON).
3. Scan a barcode or QR code.
4. If the code exists, the product info is loaded automatically.
5. If **not found**, you can add it manually or delete scanned items.
6. In **Advanced Mode**:
   - Enter prices, manufacture, and expiry dates.
   - Receive alerts for expiring products.
7. Export your product list or updated database to CSV.
8. Use the toolbar **Close button** to exit.
